CALL FOR PAPERS

Dear Readers and Contributors,

The journal Romanian Review of Regional Studies is currently assessing papers for publication in the two 2017 issues. Interested contributors are welcome to submit original research papers, review articles, conference announcements, book reviews that consider the topics covered by the journal, edited and typewritten according to the Submission Guidelines.

The publication of Volume XIII, Number 1, 2017 is scheduled as follows:

1. The deadline for submission of papers by contributors is 1 February 2017.
2. Notification of provisional acceptance of the paper will be sent to the corresponding author within two weeks of the reception.
3. The peer-review and the editing process will be completed by the end of April 2017.
4. The final preparation and printing of the issue will take place in May 2017.
5. The issue will then be published and uploaded on the journal’s website by the beginning of June 2017.

The publication of Volume XIII, Number 2, 2017 is scheduled as follows:

1. The deadline for submission of papers by contributors is 1 September 2017.
2. Notification of provisional acceptance of the paper will be sent to the corresponding author within two weeks of the reception.
3. The peer-review and the editing process will be completed by the end of October 2017.
4. The final preparation and printing of the issue will take place in November 2017.
5. The issue will then be published and uploaded on the journal’s website by the beginning of December 2017.

For further information and any other enquires regarding the journal, the paper submission or the reviewing process, please do not hesitate to contact the Editorial Office at:

cgrcluj@gmail.com

We look forward to publishing the results of your research!

 

Yours sincerely,
The Editorial Team
